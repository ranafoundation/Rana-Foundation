About us

Rana Foundation Trust

Rana Foundation, based in the culturally rich city of Varanasi, India, is a non-profit trust dedicated to fostering education, preserving cultural heritage, and empowering communities through innovative and inclusive initiatives. Established with a vision to create a positive and lasting impact on society, the foundation operates exclusively for charitable purposes under Section 2(15) of the Income Tax Act, 1961.

Driven by its core objectives, Rana Foundation focuses on a wide range of activities, including promoting quality education through scholarships, skill development programs, and the establishment of educational institutions. It also champions the preservation and promotion of India’s rich cultural heritage by organizing cultural events, supporting research, and facilitating national and international cultural exchanges.

The foundation is deeply committed to community engagement and public awareness, working tirelessly to bridge gaps in education, technology, and social development. By leveraging digital transformation, Rana Foundation aims to make education and cultural resources accessible to all, while also empowering underprivileged communities through digital literacy and skill development initiatives.

Through leadership development programs, research, and documentation, Rana Foundation nurtures future leaders and contributes to the advancement of knowledge. Its interdisciplinary approach integrates cultural studies with academic fields, fostering a deeper understanding and appreciation of India’s diverse traditions and heritage.

Rana Foundation stands as a beacon of hope and progress, striving to create a society where education, culture, and community development go hand in hand to build a brighter, more inclusive future for all.
