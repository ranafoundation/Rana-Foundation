Welcome to Rana Foundation

We are here to help everyone in need

Rana Foundation, based in Varanasi, India, is a non-profit trust dedicated to promoting education, preserving cultural heritage, and empowering communities. Through scholarships, skill development programs, and the establishment of educational institutions, it works to enhance access to quality education. The foundation also organizes cultural events and supports research to promote India's rich heritage. By leveraging digital transformation, it bridges gaps in education and social development, while fostering future leaders through leadership programs and research. Rana Foundation is committed to building a more inclusive, educated, and culturally enriched society.
